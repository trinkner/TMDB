From the FindAGrave volunteer:

I decided to send you an email to your photo request for John Ward in Pleasant Valley Cemetery. I went to the cemetery yesterday evening and I did find where he was buried. Sadly he is unmarked and whether a stone was ever present is unclear. But I wanted to email with what I did find out for you. Sorry that I could not help more. When I searched Section A the only Wards I found were a William Ward and Emily J Brown Ward. Luckily the cemetery has a map and list of interments alphabetically at the cemetery. William and Emily I believe are his parents. William is buried at Sec A Lot 73 Plot 1. Emily Sec A Lot 73 Plot 2. And by the papers John is Sec A Lot 73 Plot 3. If you are looking at the stone of William and Emily he should be buried to the right of that stone. From the papers at the cemetery John's birth date only showed 18_, and a death date 1936. I also wrote on paper the inscription on the stone of William and Emily. (below) This is all that I was able to find at the cemetery. I did look up a website with contact information to someone with the cemetery should you have any questions or need of records. http://bentleyks.webs.com/community.htm Scott Galloway and Asa Galloway are listed with the cemetery. Scotts number is 316-619-6290 Asa's numbers are 316-619-6289 and 316-772-5151 I hope I was helpful for your research. If you have any other questions please do not hesitate to contact me.
