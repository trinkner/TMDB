  Narrative

John H. Powell and Elizabeth "Lizzy" Dahler apparently had no children. In the 1900 federal census, they were living with Elizabeth's mother (also named Elizabeth), with no children. In the 1910 federal census, they lived together and Lizzy's entry indicates that she had had no children by 1910, a year when she was already 36 years old. They had two boarders living with them in the 1920 federal census. They lived alone, with no children, in the 1930 federal census. In the 1940 federal census, they lived together, along with Lizzy's brother, William H. Dahler, who is listed as John's brother-in-law.
