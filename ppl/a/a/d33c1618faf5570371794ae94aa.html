Helmink, Berendina

Birth Name Helmink, Berendina
Gender female
Age at Death 44 years, 6 months, 16 days

Narrative

Berendina (Bent) Helmink emigrated from Wisch, the Netherlands, with her husband and two daughters, Dirk Jan Bent, in 1856. The quickly made their way to Cleveland, as they appear in the 1860 Cleveland census.

In 1870, Berendina and Dirk were apparently living in a saloon building, where Dena was working as a saloon keeper.

Her husband may have married again in 1873, as there is a marriage record from Cleveland of a Derk John Bent marrying a Catherine Zent. If so, this suggests that Berendina died before 1873.
