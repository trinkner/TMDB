Narrative

The short life of David Hamilton Thompson was one of a the search for new opportunity in a rural corner of the United States that offered such opportunity readily. His father, an Irish immigrant, searched throughout his years for a better life, leaving Ireland, moving to Tennessee, and finally moving to Missouri. David continued this ever-moving search for a wealth, buying at least three farms in just 20 years, each further west than the last as the country expanded westward.

David was born in 1807 as the fourth of seven children. His father, also named David Thompson, was an early Irish immigrant who settled in Tennessee, where David Hamilton was born. His mother, Malinda "Millie" Neville, was a southerner from North Carolina who, after her husband's death, traveled across the United States by covered wagon with two of her sons and died in northern California at an age well over 100.

Sometime before he was 13, David's parents left Tennessee to live in the Missouri territory. David's father died in 1821, when David was just 14, a year after the territory entered the union as a slave state. David probably helped his four older brothers manage his father's farm.

On 12 July 1827, David married Anne Williams, a woman three years younger than he who probably had been married before and may have gone by the name of Anne Dorting or Darting before their marriage. David and Anne had a total of nine children.

By 1830 David and Anne had a farm of their own Callaway county. By 1840, they had moved west to Buchanan county in the Platte Purchase land that had been added to western Missouri in 1837. Likely, David was lured by the chance at cheap farm land in this newly opened area at a time when land speculation was nearly a national pastime. By 1850, David and Anne had moved to farmland in Holt county, even further to the west in Missouri, again perhaps lured by the profits to be had in selling their existing farm and buying a new one.

David appears to have been a farmer throughout his life. Sadly, he died at the age of just 46 in Holt county.
