Narrative

From FindAGrave:

Charles W. Trinkner died July 16, 1954, residence, 4321 Howlett Ave., Cleveland, Ohio. His wife, Anna (nee Baylon) born 1880 died April 17, 1961 at Fairview General Hospital in Cleveland at age 81, formerly of Howlett Ave.

Charles was a former CTS conductor; member of Woodmen of the World Camp #128 and of VFW Dad's Club #10.

THEIR CHILDREN:
* Carl Trinkner
* William Trinkner
* Edward J. Trinkner born June 3, 1904 in OH, died Jan 6, 1991 St John West Shore Hospital, Westlake, OH, age 86, his wife surviving
* Mrs Hilda Deas Rachine born 1902 in Cleveland, married at age 18, Jan 1 1920, Robert Deas age 21. He was a painter, born abt 1899 in Cleveland the son of James Deas & Mary Sluhan.

CHARLES' SIBLINGS:
* Mrs Lena Haag
* Mrs Clara Stevens

ANNA'S SISTER: Mrs Mary Schneider

Charles' funeral was at Corrigan's Funeral Home, Lorain Ave. at W. 148 St. Monday, July 19, 1954;
Both were buried in Brooklyn Heights Cemetery.

Sources: Plain Dealer; Cleveland Necrology File, Reel #162.
