Buckham, Andrew

Birth Name Buckham, Andrew
Gender male
Age at Death 63 years, 10 months, 20 days

Narrative

Andrew Buckham, was born in Scotland and immigrated to the eastern American south. He worked as a cabinet maker. He married Charlotte Taylor, who was from Maryland but of Welsh parentage.

Around 1815, Andrew and Charlotte moved with their children from Tennessee to Hopkins County, Kentucky.
