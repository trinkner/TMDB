Author Richard Trinkner, born 1917, native of Freudental, Germany
Publication information Created by Richard Trinkner, born 1917, a native resident of Freudental, Germany. Richard was an avid genealogist, presumably working with the church and civic records in his native language, German. The document was copied by his grandson, Roland Trinkner, and sent in hardcopy form to their distant cousin Roger Trinkner in the United States. Although the stammliste is not an official document, I have found no errors in it yet, and have cross-checked many references with the church records of Freudental.
