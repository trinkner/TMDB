Massachusetts Births and Christenings 1639-1915

Author FamilySearch
Publication information http://FamilySearch.org. Index based upon data collected by the Genealogical Society of Utah, Salt Lake City.

Narrative

Name index to birth, baptism and christening records from the state of Massachusetts. Microfilm copies of these records are available at the Family History Library and Family History Centers. This set contains 5,973,657 records. Due to privacy laws, recent records may not be displayed. The year range represents most of the records. A few records may be earlier or later. Accessed via http://search.labs.familysearch.org.
